About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Innovations in Science and Engineering (RISE-2025) aims to provide a platform for researchers, academicians, scientists, and industry professionals to present their findings, exchange ideas, and discuss recent trends in science, engineering, and technology.

Organized by Government Engineering College Siwan under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of Bihar, this event will bring together multidisciplinary experts from across the world to share research and innovative practices fostering sustainable development.

Conference Themes

  • Advances in Applied Sciences, Engineering, and Technology
  • Innovations in Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science
  • Sustainable and Green Engineering Solutions
  • Emerging Technologies for Smart Cities and Environment
  • Role of Mathematics in Engineering and Applied Research

Who Can Participate

  • Students, Research Scholars, and Academicians
  • Scientists, Engineers, and Innovators
  • Industry Professionals and Entrepreneurs
  • Government and NGO Representatives
  • Anyone interested in science, innovation, and sustainable technology

Expected Outcomes

  • Global collaboration and knowledge exchange in science and engineering.
  • Exposure to new trends and innovations across multiple disciplines.
  • Opportunities for publication and research networking.
  • Enhanced participation of students and professionals in R&D.
