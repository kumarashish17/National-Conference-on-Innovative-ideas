About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Innovations in Science and Engineering (RISE-2025) aims to bring together leading academics, researchers, industry professionals, and students to share insights and research innovations in science and engineering.

Organized by Government Engineering College Siwan under DSTTE (Government of Bihar), the conference promotes multidisciplinary research and collaborative advancements.
