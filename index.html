About the Conference

National Mathematics Day is celebrated every year on December 22 to honour the birth anniversary of the great mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan. To commemorate this occasion, Government Engineering College Siwan is organizing the National Conference on Recent Innovations in Science and Engineering (RISE-2025).

The conference aims to promote interdisciplinary research, showcase innovative projects, and bring together experts, researchers, academicians, and students to discuss the latest advancements in science, technology, and engineering.

Conference Themes

  • Advances in Applied Sciences, Engineering, and Technology
  • Sustainable Approaches for Environmental Protection
  • Role of Mathematics in Engineering and Research
  • Innovations in Computing, AI, and Smart Technologies
  • Pure and Applied Mathematics

Who Can Participate

  • Undergraduate and Postgraduate Students
  • Research Scholars and Academicians
  • Industry Professionals
  • Scientists and Innovators
  • Entrepreneurs and Technology Enthusiasts

Expected Outcomes

  • Encouraging innovation and research collaboration among academia and industry.
  • Providing a platform for presenting recent advancements in science and engineering.
  • Facilitating networking between researchers, students, and professionals.
  • Highlighting sustainable and technology-driven development practices.
